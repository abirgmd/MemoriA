What is MCI?

Understanding Mild
Cognitive Impairment

Mild Cognitive Impairment (MCI) causes cognitive changes noticed by the person affected and those around them — but does not yet prevent everyday activities.

MCI can develop for multiple reasons. Some individuals progress to dementia; others remain stable or revert to normal cognition. Early detection dramatically improves treatment outcomes.
